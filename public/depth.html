Basis & limitations

  • This page is fully editable and saves to your own storage — click any card, filled or vacant, to edit or clear it. Changes persist across sessions in the same way as the Squad Book Value and PSR Ledger tools.
  • CA (Current Ability) is FM's internal ability rating, useful for relative squad-strength comparison but not a real-world scouting metric.
  • Several players in the Out list are the same names tracked in the Squad Book Value tool. Worth checking whether any need a disposal entry in the PSR Ledger once fees are confirmed, since their book values keep amortising until recorded as sold.
  • Vacant slots reflect depth-chart placeholders for uncovered positions/roles, not an assertion that no player can play there.
