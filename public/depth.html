Basis & limitations

  • This page is fully editable and saves to your own storage — click any card, filled or vacant, to edit or clear it. Changes persist across sessions in the same way as the Squad Book Value and PSR Ledger tools.
  • Drag any card onto another — within a row or across different position rows — to swap who occupies each slot. The "1st/2nd/3rd choice" label stays fixed to the slot; only the player data moves, so dragging your 2nd-choice left-back onto the 1st-choice slot promotes them and demotes the other player in one move. Works with vacant slots too.
  • Flags and photos: nationality flags render automatically from the nationality field. Photos need a URL you paste in yourself under "Photo URL" in the edit form — I can't source or embed real player photos directly, since that runs into copyright (club/photographer-owned images) regardless of framing. Without a URL, players get a generated initials avatar instead.
  • Pitch view maps each position row onto a formation-shaped layout by matching keywords in the row's label (e.g. "Right Back" → right-back spot). Renaming a row to something the matcher doesn't recognise drops it into an "Unmapped" strip below the pitch — rename it back toward football terminology to restore its position. Drag-and-drop only works in list view for now.
  • CA (Current Ability) is FM's internal ability rating, useful for relative squad-strength comparison but not a real-world scouting metric.
  • Several players in the Out list are the same names tracked in the Squad Book Value tool. Worth checking whether any need a disposal entry in the PSR Ledger once fees are confirmed, since their book values keep amortising until recorded as sold.
  • Vacant slots reflect depth-chart placeholders for uncovered positions/roles, not an assertion that no player can play there.
