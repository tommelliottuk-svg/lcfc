Basis & limitations

  • P&S/SCR and SCMP are mutually exclusive by division. The rolling three-year Adjusted EBT test only applies in the Championship and Premier League; League One and League Two run on SCMP instead — a single-season wages-vs-turnover test with no rolling component. Set each season's "Level" in the Season Inputs table and the tool greys out whichever panel doesn't apply that year.
  • Adjusted EBT here = Revenue − Wages − Amortisation − Other opex + Player trading profit/loss + Add-backs. Real statutory accounts also net off interest, player loan income, and other smaller lines not modelled here — treat this as directional, not audit-ready.
  • Amortisation per season is computed live from the squad tracker's contract data, and is truncated at a player's recorded sale date so no further charge accrues after disposal — new signings added to the squad tracker will automatically flow through here.
  • Seasons run 1 July–30 June, matching the contract expiry convention in your data. If Leicester's actual financial year end differs, the season attribution of amortisation and disposals will need adjusting.
  • Squad purchases (rather than disposals) don't need a separate entry here — add the player to the Squad Book Value tracker and their amortisation will be picked up automatically in the season they're signed.
