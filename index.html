Basis, source & limitations

  • Source data: Leicester City contract records exported from the Football Manager database (transfer fee, currency, exchange rate, contract start/end/expiry per contract period). Fees in non-GBP currencies are converted at the recorded exchange rate.
  • These are FM database fees, not audited club accounts — treat as a working proxy for real book value, not the club's actual capitalised cost, particularly for deals with add-ons or agent fees the database doesn't separate out.
  • Players showing £0 cost are free transfers or academy graduates — any sale of these players is recognised as pure profit against PSR, which is correct treatment, not a data gap.
  • Renewal contract periods are re-amortised over the new remaining term from the point of signing, per standard EFL/Premier League club accounting treatment. No additional capitalised cost is assumed on renewal unless you add one manually.
  • Edits you make here are saved in your browser only (not shared, not sent anywhere) and will persist next time you open this file in the same browser. Use "Reset to uploaded data" to discard edits and reload the original export.
